| rule | support | confidence | lift |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| beer -> diapers | 0.2750 | 1.0000 | 3.0769 |

| eggs -> milk | 0.3000 | 1.0000 | 1.9139 |

| butter -> bread | 0.4400 | 1.0000 | 1.6949 |
